Creating Your Own Personal Sport Homepage

To create your personal sport home page, go to our Member Options feature and view the blue links titled Personal Home Pages & Create Personal Home Page. Select your preferences; review pricing for Options (1) & (2); (note: Option (3) is for customized home pages). Please answer the questions requested then proceed to the “Go to Payment” link to complete your order...If you need assistance, contact us at support@k2ps.com (Attn: PHP)

Please use your PC / tablet to view complete Member Option details.
