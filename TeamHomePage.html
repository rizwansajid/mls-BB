Creating Your Own Team Sport Home Page

MyLifeSports offers the opportunity to its members to create exclusive Team Sports Memory Home Pages. There are two options which can be reviewed on your PC / tablets by going to the Member Options page and clicking on the Blue Link titled “Create Team Pages”. If you need further assistance, contact us at mlshelp@optimum.net (Attn: Teams)
